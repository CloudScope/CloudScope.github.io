Why CloudScope?

I've been on both sides of the DevOps interview table.

As a Sr. DevOps Engineer at JP Morgan Chase — one of the world's largest financial institutions — I work at a scale where infrastructure reliability isn't optional. Kubernetes clusters running thousands of pods, Terraform managing hundreds of cloud resources, CI/CD pipelines shipping code dozens of times a day.

I've seen what separates candidates who get offers from those who don't. It's rarely about memorising definitions. It's about understanding why things work the way they do — and being able to reason through real production scenarios under pressure.

CloudScope is my way of closing that gap. Every question here is one I've either been asked, asked myself, or answered in a real interview setting. The goal is simple: give you the depth and confidence to walk into any DevOps interview and own it.
