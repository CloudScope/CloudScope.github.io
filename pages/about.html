About Me

I'm Raghunath Pradhan, a DevOps Engineer passionate about cloud-native technologies and infrastructure automation. I work extensively with Kubernetes, Docker, AWS, Terraform, and CI/CD pipelines to build reliable, scalable systems.

I created CloudScope as a place to document my learning journey — the concepts I'm studying, the problems I'm solving, and the knowledge I want to retain and share. Whether you're preparing for a DevOps interview or exploring cloud technologies, I hope this blog helps you along the way.

What I Write About

My posts focus on practical DevOps topics: container orchestration with Kubernetes, containerization with Docker, cloud infrastructure on AWS, Infrastructure as Code with Terraform, and CI/CD pipeline design. I also curate interview Q&A based on real questions I've encountered, to help engineers prepare for technical interviews with confidence.
